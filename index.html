about

Yiwen Lu is an enthusiastic photographer from Shanghai, and now in New York since 2018. She just graduated and got Bachelor of Fine Arts in Photography and Video from the School of Visual Arts.

She has worked as an operation intern at Leica Akademie Greater China, Shanghai in 2021 and freelance photographer at New York Food Map in 18-2019. She was an intern at L. Parker Stephenson Photographs in 2023 spring.
She photographs events, building interiors, food, still life, and portraits.
